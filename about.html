Who We Are

Trust & Quality Since 1972

Aruna & Co is a premier authorised dealer of Tamil Nadu Newsprint & Papers Ltd (TNPL), a name synonymous with quality and sustainability in the paper industry. With a rich legacy spanning over five decades, we have established ourselves as a cornerstone in the paper distribution network across Tamil Nadu.

Our journey began in 1972 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality paper products with unmatched reliability. Today, we stand proud as a preferred partner for:

  • Corporate Offices & Institutions
  • Commercial Printing Presses
  • Educational Establishments
  • Wholesale & Retail Stationery Suppliers

We are committed to eco-friendly practices, proudly distributing TNPL's bagasse-based paper products that minimize environmental impact without compromising on quality. Our dedicated logistics team ensures timely delivery, ensuring that your business operations never face a downtime due to stock unavailability.

Sustainability

Eco-Friendly Paper Production

TNPL is a pioneer in manufacturing paper from bagasse, an agricultural residue. By choosing TNPL products, you are supporting a sustainable and eco-friendly paper industry that minimizes environmental impact.

We ensure that our distribution process maintains the integrity and quality of these premium papers right to your doorstep.
