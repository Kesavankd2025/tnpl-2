OVERVIEW

Product Overview

TNPL Platinum is a premium copier paper engineered for superior aesthetics and performance. With high whiteness (155 CIE) and exceptional smoothness, it delivers sharp, vibrant prints perfect for corporate stationery, reports, and presentations. It is the ideal choice for businesses seeking a professional edge in their documentation.
